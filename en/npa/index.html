The Independent Police Union (NPA) represents police employees, including officers with special ranks, to protect their social, economic, and professional interests.

Collective Agreement with the State Police

Original signed document of the collective agreement between NPA and the State Police

The collective agreement signed on 19 October 2006 between NPA and the State Police remains in force and applies to both employees working under employment contracts and officers with special ranks.
